About me

My drive and learning

Over time, my interests shifted toward software development, particularly the user interface. Through various work experiences, I witnessed firsthand the importance of designing software that not only functions but also provides a seamless user experience. This realization has shaped my career path, and I am committed to crafting applications that are both visually appealing and user-friendly.

As a developer

I am a skilled software engineer with a strong foundation in Angular and a passion for crafting user-centric solutions. My goal is to continue expanding my skill set to become a full-stack engineer, delivering exceptional digital experiences.

When we're united by a common goal and support each other, there's no limit to what we can achieve.
